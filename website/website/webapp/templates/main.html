Get in touch

Once you’ve found a vehicle which interests you, contact us. This can be via telephone, email or by visiting one of our showrooms. One of our dedicated customer advisers will be on hand to answer any questions; to organise a part-exchange deal if needed; and to arrange finance if required. Once a deal has been agreed, our adviser will be on hand to assist you every step of the way to ensure a smooth, transparent handover.

Collect your car or take deliver

If you're coming to the showroom, we’ll arrange a convenient time for you to pick up and drive away your new car. If you have taken advantage of our delivery service, then we will be in touch to organise a delivery at your convenience. On the day of delivery, rest assured that our driver will not leave until you have inspected your vehicle and are completely happy with everything. If there is a part-exchange involved, our driver will take away your old vehicle.
