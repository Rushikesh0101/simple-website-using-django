About CarSuperMarket

CarSuperMarket is a online used car supermarket that provides car sales and services.

Our aim is to supply high quality cars, at prices that are checked daily to ensure they remain competitive, whilst delivering a great service and experience that keeps our customer retention rate as high as possible.We have over 3,000 cars in group stock and sell over 1600 cars a month. Our plan is to increase these numbers over the next 3-5 years to establish ourselves as one of the UK's largest and most successful car supermarkets.
